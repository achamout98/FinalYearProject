Start:
	Player walks up to the wall/mountain
	option to look around and decide on which path to take
	Start climbing; as soon as the player grabs a rock, the game starts

Functionality:
	Player can only climb up one rock at a time
	Player can swing sideways to gain momentum for juming sideways
	Grip strength, slowly depletes while player is hanging on to a rock
	Grip strength forces players to keep moving

End:
	The game ends once the player either finishes the level or falls
	The player can fall by either losing grip strength by staying stationary for too long or by missing a rock
	jump.
